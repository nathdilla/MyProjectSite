#1 - Strawberry Fields Forever
In my opinion this the greatest Beatles song of all time. Lennon paints this dreamy scene from his childhood through his lyrics. McCartney's melotron intro is the most captivating intro to any song ever. Ringo's drumming is really great along with Harrison's accompaniment.
#2 - Day in the Life
McCartney and Lennon combine their lyrical masterminds to create this masterpiece of a song. Complete with a real orchestra and the haunting voice of Lennon juxtaposed to McCartney's portion of the song makes this one of their best ever.
#3 - In My Life
George Harrison's opening riff just works well with the song. The song is written beautifully, the chord progression is captivating and the boroque-piano section is something I thouroughly enjoy and greatly appreciative of.
